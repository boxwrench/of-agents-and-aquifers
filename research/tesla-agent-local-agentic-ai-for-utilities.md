## Summary
Welcome to **tesla_agent**! This repository is a clean-room, plug-and-play template designed to teach water treatment operators and utility professionals how to build and run local, private agentic AI workflows on consumer AMD hardware.

## 💧 Relevance to Of Agents and Aquifers
*Why is this important for water systems, agentic modeling, or utilities?*
- Serve as the foundational codebase and testing framework for deploying local water-utility AI agents.
- Demonstrates ROCm/Vulkan serving and local safety gates for water systems.

## 🤖 Agentic Aspects
*What autonomous or intelligence features does this research touch upon?*
- Focuses on local models executing operations (tool calling, plan reviews).
- Implements safety checkpoints (Chapter 11) for executing tools on telemetry/SCADA systems.
